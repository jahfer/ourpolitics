The Liberal Party of Canada has introduced legislation to criminalize the intentional obstruction of access to places of worship, schools, and community centers, as well as the intimidation or threat of attendees at these locations. This policy aims to protect public spaces and ensure safety, addressing emerging forms of harm both physically and digitally[^1][^2]. The legislation includes new criminal offenses for sharing non-consensual sexual deepfake images, highlighting a comprehensive approach to safeguarding individuals from harassment and digital exploitation[^2].
